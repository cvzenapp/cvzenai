# Referral Creation Guide

## Getting Started with Referrals

Welcome to CVZen's referral system! This guide will walk you through creating and managing referrals to earn rewards while helping your network find great job opportunities.

## What is a Referral?

A referral is when you recommend someone from your professional network for a job opportunity. When your referral gets hired, you earn a reward as a thank you for making the connection.

## How to Create a Referral

### Step 1: Access the Referral Form

1. Log into your CVZen account
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard**
3. Click on **"Create New Referral"** or go to the **Referrals** section
4. Click the **"+ New Referral"** button

### Step 2: Fill Out the Referral Information

#### Required Information

**Referee Details:**
- **Full Name**: Enter the complete name of the person you're referring
- **Email Address**: Provide a valid email where they can be reached
  - ⚠️ **Important**: Each person can only be referred once per position

**Job Details:**
- **Position Title**: The specific role you're referring them for (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
- **Company Name**: The organization with the job opening

#### Optional Information

**Additional Details:**
- **Job Description**: Paste or describe the job requirements and responsibilities
- **Expected Salary**: Salary range for the position (helps set expectations)
- **Location**: Where the job is located (remote, city, etc.)
- **Personal Message**: A personalized note to your referral (up to 1,000 characters)

### Step 3: Review and Submit

1. Double-check all information for accuracy
2. Review your personal message for tone and clarity
3. Click **"Send Referral"**
4. You'll receive a confirmation that your referral was created successfully

## What Happens Next?

### Immediate Actions
1. **Email Sent**: Your referral receives an email invitation with:
   - Job details you provided
   - Your personal message
   - Link to respond to the referral
   - Information about the opportunity

2. **Referral Tracking**: The referral appears in your dashboard with status "Pending"

### Referral Journey
Your referral will progress through these stages:

1. **Pending** - Referral created, waiting for response
2. **Contacted** - Your referral has responded with interest
3. **Interviewed** - Interview process has begun
4. **Hired** - Your referral got the job! 🎉 (Reward earned)
5. **Rejected** - Unfortunately, it didn't work out this time

## Best Practices for Successful Referrals

### Choose the Right People
- **Relevant Skills**: Ensure they have the qualifications for the role
- **Career Interest**: Consider if they're actively looking or open to opportunities
- **Professional Relationship**: Refer people you know professionally and can vouch for

### Write Effective Personal Messages
```
Hi Sarah,

I came across this Senior Marketing Manager position at TechCorp and immediately thought of you. Given your experience leading campaigns at StartupXYZ and your expertise in digital marketing, I think you'd be a perfect fit.

The role involves leading a team of 5 marketers and developing go-to-market strategies for their new product line. The company culture is very collaborative and they offer excellent growth opportunities.

Would you be interested in learning more? I'd be happy to make an introduction!

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Timing Considerations
- **Don't Rush**: Take time to craft a thoughtful message
- **Consider Their Situation**: Respect if someone recently started a new job
- **Follow Up Appropriately**: You can check in, but don't be pushy

## Earning Rewards

### Reward Structure
- **Standard Positions**: $500 reward for successful hires
- **Senior/Specialized Roles**: Up to $1,500 for high-level positions
- **Bonus Opportunities**: Special campaigns may offer increased rewards

### Payout Process
1. **Reward Earned**: When your referral is hired, you earn the reward
2. **Pending Period**: 90-day waiting period to ensure successful placement
3. **Payout Request**: Request payout when you reach the minimum threshold ($100)
4. **Payment Processing**: Receive payment via PayPal, bank transfer, or check

### Tax Considerations
- Rewards over $600 per year require tax reporting (1099 form)
- Keep records of your referral earnings
- Consult a tax professional for specific advice

## Common Questions

### Can I refer the same person multiple times?
Yes, but only for different positions. You cannot refer the same person for the same role twice.

### What if my referral doesn't respond?
- The system sends automatic follow-up emails
- You can reach out personally to encourage response
- Referrals expire after 90 days if no response

### Can I refer someone I don't know personally?
It's best to refer people you know professionally. Referrals work best when there's a genuine connection and you can vouch for their abilities.

### What if the hiring process takes a long time?
- Referrals remain active throughout the hiring process
- You'll receive updates as the status changes
- Rewards are earned when the final hiring decision is made

### Can I withdraw a referral?
Yes, you can withdraw a referral before the person is hired. Contact support or use the dashboard to manage your referrals.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Email already referred" error:**
- This person has already been referred for this position
- Try referring them for a different role
- Check if you've referred them recently

**Referral not receiving emails:**
- Verify the email address is correct
- Check their spam/junk folder
- Ask them to whitelist emails from CVZen

**Status not updating:**
- Updates may take 24-48 hours to reflect
- Contact the hiring team if you have concerns
- Reach out to support for assistance
